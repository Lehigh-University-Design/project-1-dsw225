Browser Wars

1990

WorldWideWeb - later renamed Nexus becomes the first web browser

1994 - 1995

Netscape, and Internet Explorer are released. The “wars” were not solely about technical features, but also involved strategic business decisions and legal disputes.

2002

As Internet Explorer becomes more dominant and popular in the wars, Mozilla Firefox is introduced and gains popularity quickly due to its speed and security.

2003

With the release of Mac OS X Panther, the operating system was bundled with Safari, and Safari gains popularity.

2008

Google Chrome is introduced, and quickly becomes one of the most popular browsers due to its speed and simplicity.

2013

Microsoft Edge becomes Microsofts premier browser and is built off the Chromium (Chrome) Engine.
